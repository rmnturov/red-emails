What is GrowthBot

GrowthBot is a chatbot for marketing and sales professionals -- or anyone that's working on growing a business (like startup folks). It connects to a variety of marketing systems (think HubSpot, Google Analytics, etc.) and gives you quick, easy access to information and services.

Example Questions

How much do companies spend on marketing? What PPC keywords is Dropbox.com buying? What marketing software does Zendesk.com use? Those are just a few of the powerful things you can ask GrowthBot.
